Alisa Mikhailovna Kujou

アリサ・ミハイロヴナ・九条

The Solitary Princess

A first-year student at Seirei Private Academy, honor student with the highest grades, and the Student Council Treasurer. Known for her ethereal beauty and tsundere personality.

Personality

Academic Excellence

Always ranks first in class tests, excels in sports, and serves as Student Council Treasurer since her first year. She's an honor student with excellent grades and good conduct.

Tsundere Nature

Often teases Masachika Kuze and is a possessive type who gets jealous easily, especially when he interacts with other girls. She's hopelessly in love with him but often unaware of her intense feelings.

Beauty & Pride

Understands that beauty is a weapon in interpersonal relationships and is very proud of her appearance, which rivals any celebrity. She doesn't use makeup due to school regulations but maintains her natural beauty.

Independence

Doesn't particularly care about others, for better or worse. She's known for being exceptionally hard on her neighbor in class, who represents the school's unseriousness.

Background

Early Life

Comes from a well-off family with privileges and a nurturing environment. Despite being handed advantages in life, she's determined to be defined by her efforts and always striving for the top.

Time in Vladivostok

Her cold and rigid personality developed during her time in Vladivostok, where disappointment and lack of motivation from classmates made her realize their ignorance, creating unconscious condescension within her.

Return to Japan

In her third year of junior high school, she returned to Japan due to her father's work. On her parents' recommendation, she transferred to Seirei Educational Institute, a prestigious school considered one of the best in Japan.

Academic Success

Despite being away from Japan for 5 years, she scored first place in her school year on the proficiency test right after transferring, instantly making her popular among students.
